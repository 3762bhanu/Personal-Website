I design and build backend systems in Java and Python, with an emphasis on correctness, maintainability, and performance. I have experience building REST APIs, event-driven services, and batch processing pipelines.

#### Distributed Systems

I have hands-on experience with the challenges of distributed systems: consistency, fault tolerance, service discovery, and distributed tracing. I understand the trade-offs involved and how to make pragmatic decisions under real constraints.

#### Platform & Infrastructure

I work regularly with Kubernetes for container orchestration, including managing deployments, configuring resource limits, and debugging production incidents. I am comfortable with CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and GitOps workflows.

#### Databases & Data Access

I have deep experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and have worked with NoSQL stores and message queues (Kafka, Redis). I pay careful attention to query performance, indexing strategy, and connection management.

#### Observability

I believe that a system is not production-ready until it can be understood in production. I build systems with structured logging, meaningful metrics, distributed tracing, and alerting as first-class concerns.
